STEP 1

Discovery

We begin the project by asking many questions to understand your business, product, and users better. This is an important step to get everyone on the same page and make effective project plans.

01. WORKSHOP

Conduct a workshop involving all stakeholders to collect product information, industry insights, establish project objectives, and identify target users.

02.RESEARCH

Each project undergoes our standard research process, which includes analyzing the current market situation, studying competitors, and understanding user needs.

03. WORKSHOP

Once we thoroughly understand the business and users, we identify problems, set goals, and create a plan for brainstorming and generating ideas

STEP 2

Ideation

During this stage, we engage in brainstorming sessions to address specific challenges and generate innovative ideas for incorporating new product features. Our focus is on aligning these ideas with user tasks and assessing their technical feasibility to ensure practicality and effectiveness.

01. USERFLOWS

Organize a workshop with all relevant stakeholders to gather product details, gain insights into the industry, set project goals, and pinpoint target users.

02. INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE

Prioritizing the needs and preferences of users when designing the information architecture. User research and usability testing are essential in this process.

03. WIREFRAMES

Creating the product's structure involves defining its architecture and organizing all the functionalities and features. This process helps you conceptualize and visualize the final product.

STEP 3

Design

We craft visually appealing interfaces that align seamlessly with the brand's established style. Additionally, we develop interactive prototypes to pinpoint any design weaknesses and iterate based on feedback, ensuring a refined and user-friendly final product.

01. MOODBOARD

We're creating visual ideas based on the brand and moodboards. When we agree on a style, we'll use it for all the screens to make everything look consistent and appealing.

02. VISUAL DESIGN

We're brainstorming visual ideas based on the brand and mood boards. When we agree on a style, we'll apply it to all screens.

03. DESIGN SYSTEM

We're creating detailed documentation for developers and our design teams, covering colors, typography, and design components.

STEP 4

Deliver

This is the last step where we transfer all assets and knowledge to the product/tech team for implementation.

01. HAND OFF

We offer a concise document with clear instructions, accompanied by the design system, to simplify the development process.

02. UI ANIMATIONS

We provide Lottie files and videos for developers to easily implement delightful animations in the final product.

03. PROTOTYPE & TESTING

We conduct design prototype testing at different stages, from wireframes to visual design, to validate our assumptions and design choices.
